Campaign Performance Project

In this project, we were asked to assess the performance of campaigns from data given to us by our mentors. The Data we gained was a Dataset consist of 2 years transaction report, and the transaction also divided into 3 cities. The mentor need insights from:

Problems

  • Acquisition Performance of new live merchants (Merchants with minimum one successful transaction) in January and February
  • Retention Rate of last year's merchants
  • Participation Rate of Merchant Promo
  • Should the campaign be continue, or we need to prioritize a city to do the campaign

Objectives Required

  • Acquire a minimum of 150 new live merchants every month
  • Keep the retention rate a minimum of 85% of last year's existing merchants
  • Increase promo discount participation rate to a minimum of 30% of all merchants
